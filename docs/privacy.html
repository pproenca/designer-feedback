Privacy Policy

Product: Designer Feedback (Chrome extension) Effective date: January 27, 2026

Designer Feedback stores your annotations and settings locally on your device. We do not operate any servers and do not collect analytics.

Information we handle

The extension runs on pages you visit and helps you capture feedback. The data below is processed locally to provide the core functionality:

  • Web history: page URL and title for the annotation you create.
  • User activity: clicks, annotation positions, and timestamps.
  • Website content: element labels/paths and page screenshots when you export a report.

How we use this information

  • To place and render annotations on the current page.
  • To generate exports (HTML, image, or ZIP) when you choose to export.
  • To show an annotation count badge.

Storage and syncing

Annotations are stored locally in your browser storage. Extension settings are stored using Chrome Sync (chrome.storage.sync), which may sync those settings across your Chrome profiles if you have sync enabled. We do not access or process this data outside your browser.

Sharing

We do not sell, transfer, or share your data with third parties. Data is only exported when you choose to download a report to your device.

Retention

Data remains in your browser storage until you delete it using the extension or clear your browser data.

Security

We keep all processing local to your device. No remote servers or analytics are used.

Your choices

  • Disable the extension on specific sites using the site list controls.
  • Clear all annotations from the extension UI.
  • Uninstall the extension to remove its local data.

Children's privacy

This extension is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes

If we update this policy, we will update the effective date at the top of this page.
