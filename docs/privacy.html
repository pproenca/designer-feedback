Privacy Policy

Product: Designer Feedback (Chrome extension) Effective date: January 27, 2026

Designer Feedback is an on-page annotation tool. It stores your annotations and settings locally on your device and only uses data needed to provide its user-facing features. We do not operate servers and do not collect analytics.

Information we collect

We process the following data on your device to provide the extension’s features:

  • Web history: page URL and title for each annotated page.
  • User activity: clicks, annotation positions, and timestamps.
  • Website content: element labels/paths and page screenshots when you export.

How we collect and use data

We collect and use this data only to deliver the extension’s single purpose:

  • Create, display, and manage annotations on the current page.
  • Generate exports (HTML, image, or ZIP) when you choose to export.
  • Show an annotation count badge.

Web browsing activity (page URL/title) is used only to associate annotations with the page you are actively annotating.

Permissions used: The extension requests tabs to read the current page URL and title, storage to save data locally, activeTab and scripting to inject the toolbar and capture screenshots on demand, downloads and offscreen to generate exports, and contextMenus to provide a right-click export shortcut.

Sharing and disclosure

We do not sell, transfer, or share user data with third parties. The only time data leaves the extension is when you choose to download an export to your own device.

Parties data is shared with: None.

Storage, syncing, and retention

Annotations are stored locally in your browser storage. Extension settings are stored using Chrome Sync (chrome.storage.sync), which may sync those settings across your Chrome profiles if you have sync enabled. We do not access or process this data outside your browser.

Data remains in your browser storage until you delete it using the extension or clear your browser data.

Security

We keep all processing local to your device. No remote servers or analytics are used.

Chrome Web Store compliance

The use of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Chrome Web Store User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

Your choices

  • Disable the extension on specific sites using the site list controls.
  • Clear all annotations from the extension UI.
  • Uninstall the extension to remove its local data.

Children's privacy

This extension is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes

If we update this policy, we will update the effective date at the top of this page.
